$d_{ij} = \| r_{i} - r_{j} \|$  : 行人中心间的距离， $r_{i}, r_{j}$为矢量

$g(x) = \left\{\begin{array}{cc}  x, & touch\ each\ other\\  0, & d_{ij} > r_{ij}, do\ not \ touch\ each\ other \end{array}\right.$
